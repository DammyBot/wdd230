Our Purpose

The purpose of Ibadan chamber of commerce website is to make the navigations of visitors around Ibadan easy and without stress. Ibadan is the capital city of Oyo state and also the largest city in West Africa till date. It's a city with so many wonderful sites like the first storey building in Nigeria, mountains and hiking sites, fancy bridges and flyovers, a 15,000 and a 20,000 capacity soccer stadium for international and local matches, and so much more.

This website will allow visitors and residents to easily navigate their way to Ibadan's top places like malls, dinners, markets, hotels, recreational sites and more. The user would be able to select categories and filter out the best using the ratings features and pricing. Amazing pictures of the location would also make it easy for recognition once they arrive. Lastly, a map feature that would allow distance calculation and easy navigation.

The domain name found available at the time of development is ibadanchambercommerce.com
